About These Materials

These resources integrate transformative learning theory, design thinking methodology, and Self-Determination Theory to help students build meaningful, sustainable careers in music. Rather than measuring success against distant aspirations, this framework emphasizes short and medium-term goals that create regular wins, build confidence through accumulated success, and construct the scaffolding that makes long-term vision possible.

The materials are designed for entrepreneurship and music industry courses at the University of St. Thomas, where students learn to align professional activities with personal values, intrinsic motivations, and desired community impact.

Core Principles

Evidence-Based

Grounded in peer-reviewed research on motivation, goal-setting, and sustainable career development.

Student-Centered

Emphasizes personal reflection, authentic goal-setting, and individualized career pathways.

Practical Application

Bridges academic theory with real-world music industry practices and entrepreneurial mindsets.

Getting Started

Ready to begin mapping your music career? Start with the Career Goal Map Worksheet. Set aside 60-90 minutes for your initial work, though you can save and return as many times as needed. The worksheet guides you through four key sections, each building on the previous one.

Before you begin, review the Career Framework Infographic to understand the three foundational questions that will guide your planning: What lights you up? What won't you trade away? Who is this for?
